Little Penguins - Privacy notice

This privacy notice sets out how we collect, use and disclose personal information collected through the Little Penguins Observations website.

The Department of Biodiversity, Conservation and Attractions (DBCA) collects this personal information to have a record of:

  • Who has logged into the website
  • Who has recorded observations of Little Penguins in videos

The only personal information we collect is first name, last name and email address.

We may share this information with other organisations involved in penguin conservation to clarify any data recorded.

For further details on how DBCA manages your personal information, you can read our Privacy Policy. If you have any questions about how your personal information will be handled or if you would like to access your personal information, please contact privacy@dbca.wa.gov.au.
